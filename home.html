Django project

This is a Django project for sale prediction. Utilizing the advertising dataset, apply a basic linear regression model to examine the relationship between "sales" and "TV, radio, and newspaper advertising." Businesses can gain important insights into the anticipated effects of their advertising strategies with the help of the predictive sales model. This gives decision-makers the ability to maximize their marketing expenditures, distribute resources wisely, and improve overall sales results.
